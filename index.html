Four Idiots

Stilton Rian Brickheg also known as Summer. He was called such because of his ability to produce and control fire.
The son of the Head of Ralexis Academy.

Ian Carlos Monteverde also known as Spring. He was called such because of his ability to control the element Earth and communicate to animals.
The son of the Duke Arman Lexior Monteverde and Duchess Dianna Rose Monteverde of Stars Kingdom.

Regon Xin Moridello also known as Fall. He was called such because of his ability to control the air and wind. He also possess the power of spellcasting.
The only child of the Councilors' head leader Miss Claire Maxyn Moridello.

Saldrin Wyborne Polederfrel also known as Winter. He was called such because of his ability to control and produce water as well as turning them into ice.
The son of the Duke Engreeve John Polederfrel and Duchess Harmane Samantha Polederfrel of Elements Kingdom. Read more...
